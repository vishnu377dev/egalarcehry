Kia Seltos New Variants 2024: Is the Gravity Edition Worth Your Money?

By: VishTech Editorial Team Updated: Nov 2024
"I remember the first time I drove the Kia Seltos back in 2019. It felt like the future. Today, even with so many rivals like the Hyundai Creta and Tata Curvv, the Seltos still manages to surprise me. The new 'Gravity Edition' is a perfect example of how Kia listens to its customers by adding premium features to the mid-variants."

The **Kia Seltos** has always been a top choice for SUV buyers in India. In 2024, Kia has updated the Seltos lineup by introducing new variants and features to stay ahead of the competition. The most exciting update is the launch of the **Gravity Edition**, which sits right in the middle of the lineup.

1. What's New in the Gravity Edition?

Kia launched the Gravity Edition to give premium features to buyers who don't want to spend on the top-end GT Line. Here are the main highlights:

  • Built-in Dashcam: For extra safety and recording your trips.
  • Ventilated Front Seats: A lifesaver for the hot Indian summer.
  • Bose Premium Sound: High-quality 8-speaker system for music lovers.
  • 17-inch Alloy Wheels: New machined designs that look very premium.
  • Electronic Parking Brake: Usually found only in luxury cars.

2. Interior and Technology

The Seltos cabin remains one of the best in its class. It features a huge dual-screen setup (10.25-inch each). One is for the driver's display, and the other is for the touchscreen. The new variants also get a massive **Panoramic Sunroof**, which makes the cabin feel very open and airy. With over 60+ connected car features, you can control many things in the car using your smartphone.

Kia Seltos Engine & Performance:
  • 🚗 1.5L Petrol: 115 PS (Perfect for City)
  • 🔥 1.5L Turbo Petrol: 160 PS (For Speed)
  • ⛽ 1.5L Diesel: 116 PS (Great Mileage)
  • ⚙️ Gears: Manual, iMT, IVT, 6AT, 7DCT
  • 🛡️ Safety: 6 Airbags (Standard on all variants)
  • ☀️ Luxury: Dual-Zone AC & Sunroof

3. Safety: ADAS Level 2

Safety is a big priority for Kia. The top variants now come with **ADAS Level 2** (Advanced Driver Assistance Systems). This includes 17 features like:

  • Forward Collision Warning: Alerts you if a crash is possible.
  • Lane Keep Assist: Helps keep the car centered in its lane.
  • Adaptive Cruise Control: Automatically adjusts speed based on traffic.
  • Blind Spot Monitoring: Warns you of cars you can't see in your mirrors.

4. Kia Seltos vs The Competition

Seltos vs Hyundai Creta: The Creta is more comfortable, but the Seltos looks more aggressive and offers a more powerful Turbo engine.

Seltos vs Maruti Grand Vitara: The Grand Vitara offers better mileage (Hybrid), but the Seltos is much more feature-rich and powerful.

5. Pros and Cons

Pros (What we like)
  • Beautiful design and premium interior.
  • Massive list of features (ADAS, Sunroof, Bose).
  • Powerful Turbo Petrol engine is fun to drive.
  • Many variant options for different budgets.
Cons (What could be better)
  • The ride can feel a bit stiff on bumpy roads.
  • Top variants are becoming very expensive.
  • The 1.5L Petrol engine feels average on highways.

Final Verdict: Should You Buy It?

The **Kia Seltos 2024** is still one of the best SUVs you can buy in India. If you want the best value for money, the new **Gravity Edition** is the one to pick. It gives you the feel of a luxury car without the price tag of the top-end model. Whether you want a family car or a powerful machine for highway trips, the Seltos has a variant for you.
