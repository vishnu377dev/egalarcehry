Mechanical Mastery:

  • Sway Bar Disconnect: Allows the front wheels to drop further for extreme rock crawling.
  • 33-inch All-Terrain Tires: Massive grip on mud, sand, and snow.
  • 800mm Water Wading: You can drive through rivers without breaking a sweat.
