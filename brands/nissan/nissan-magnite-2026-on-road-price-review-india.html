Look at it from any angle, and the Magnite doesn't look like a 'budget' car. 3. Engine & Mileage: The Fuel-Saver SUV

Nissan offers two engine choices:

  • 1.0L Natural Aspirated: Perfect for city driving and maximum mileage (Approx 18.75 kmpl).
  • 1.0L Turbo Petrol: This is the enthusiast's choice! 100 PS of power that makes the Magnite feel like a pocket rocket.

The new CNG variant is a game-changer for daily commuters, offering a real-world mileage of nearly 26 km/kg. No more worrying about rising petrol prices!

4. Safety: 4-Star Protection

Don't let the price fool you. The Magnite is built tough. It has scored a 4-star safety rating in the ASEAN NCAP crash tests.
Features include: - Electronic Stability Control (ESC) - Traction Control - Hill Start Assist - 360-degree Around View Monitor (First in segment!)

"Cheaper than a Baleno, Safest in its class. That's the Magnite promise."

5. Interior & Tech: Surprisingly Premium

Inside, you get an 8-inch touchscreen with Wireless Android Auto and Apple CarPlay. The 'floating' dashboard design looks very modern. Another cool feature is the 7-inch TFT instrument cluster that shows cool graphics when you switch modes.

Final Verdict: Should You Buy It?

If you are looking for a car under ₹10 Lakhs and safety is your priority along with looks, the **Nissan Magnite 2026** is unbeatable. It beats the Tata Punch in terms of features and the Hyundai Exter in terms of road presence.
