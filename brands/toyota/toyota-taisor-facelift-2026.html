3. Performance: The 28 kmpl Mileage Miracle

The biggest reason to buy a Toyota is fuel efficiency. The 2026 Taisor comes with a refined 1.2L Engine with Strong Hybrid Technology. This system allows the car to run on electric power at low speeds, saving you a lot of money on petrol.

The expected mileage is a staggering 28 kmpl for the hybrid variant and around 21 kmpl for the standard petrol engine. For those looking for even lower running costs, the CNG variant offers exceptional economy without compromising on performance.
